About the Founder

Likho Cekeshe

Welcome, I am Likho Cekeshe, the founder of Velvet Rose Florist.I founded Velvet Rose Florist with a singular, driving vision: to elevate the simple beauty of a bloom into a luxurious, unforgettable experience. To me, a flower isn't merely decoration; it's a tactile memory, a moment of deeply felt emotion. That's why we focus on curating not just flowers, but feelings prioritizing rich textures, deep color palettes, and the kind of intoxicating fragrance that stops you in your tracks. Every arrangement that leaves our studio, from a single, perfect Velvet Rose to a grand installation, is a testament to our commitment to unparalleled artistry and quality, ensuring every gesture sent through us feels as opulent and genuine as true affection.

Where Moments are Hand-Arranged into Masterpieces, and Every Petal is a Promise of Unforgettable, Velvet-Soft Luxury.
